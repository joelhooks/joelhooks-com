I used the 5x5 Stronglifts app on iOS to get started. It‚Äôs a simple program of 5 different lifts. You do squats, overhead presses, bench press, barbell rows, and dead lifts. The program starts you with just the bar, and stacks weight on to each of the exercises every time you encounter them.

1. Slowed down the increments that the app adds weight

![chart tracking weight lifting](./images/lifting.jpg)
